## Executive summary

The Tier-1 offensive toolbox is complete. The real 2026 gaps fall in five adjacent layers the verify-tools ledger does not cover: **modern C2**, **cloud/K8s offense**, **supply-chain/CI attack**, **structured DFIR (plaso/capa/timesketch)**, and **agentic AI-assisted VR/red-team**. Below is the merged gap list from memory + fresh April-2026 research.

## Landscape notes (April 2026)

- **Sliver** remains the OSS C2 king; Havoc is the serious runner-up.
- **SCCM / ADCS / supply-chain** have eclipsed vanilla Kerberos as the hot AD vectors. Coercer + SCCMHunter + krbrelayx are load-bearing.
- **Ludus and GOAD v3** are the new lab standard — replace ad-hoc vulnhub VMs.
- **VulnHuntr and agentic code auditors** are the real 2026 AI-security advance, not the 2024 prompt-injection-fuzzer wave.
- **BloodHound CE 6.x schema** — existing `bloodhound-python` may emit legacy JSON. Verify or switch to `bloodhound-ce`.
- **HackingBuddyGPT** — memory flagged it as blocked by PyO3 / Py3.13. Upstream fixed in v0.4 (Jan 2026). Retry install.
